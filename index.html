Dreamy Butter Chicken

image is free to use and modify from www.pexels.com credited to Saveurs Secretes, Apr 23, 2021

My Story

I spent many a sleepless night searching for the perfect butter chicken. restaurant after restaurant, many a sleepless night in search of perfection

This dreamy butter chicken recipe pairs incredibly well with coconut rice

Ingredients

  • 6 Boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 TBS butter
  • 1 large sweet yellow onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 1/2 TBS minced ginger
  • 1 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 3/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1 TBS brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 TBS curry powder
  • 1 28oz can of diced tomato
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 200ml coconut milk
  • Plain Greek yoghurt

Prep

  1. Dice the chicken into 1/2 inch pieces
  2. Dice the onions into quarter inch dice.
  3. Mince the garlic
  4. Seperate the liquid from the diced tomatoes keeping both
  5. prepare all spices and other ingredients having them ready to go

Cooking

  1. In a large pan, saute' the chicken on high heat until it has a nice golden brown color, then set it aside
  2. Heat a large pot over medium high heat, once pot is hot add butter and onions, saute' until the onions are clear and tender stirring frequently
  3. Add the ginger and garlic stirring constantly for one minute or until it has become fragrant
  4. Add the chili powder, turmeric, cumin, and curry powder and saute' for another minute
  5. Add the tomatoes, chicken stock, and coconut milk. Bring the pot to a boil stirring frequently and reduce to a simmer.
  6. Add salt, pepper, and brown sugar. continue to simmer for 15-20 minutes
  7. Using an immersion blender, blend the sauce until it is smooth
  8. Add the chicken to the sauce and simmer until chicken is fully cooked (roughly 5 minutes)
  9. Adjust the seasoning by adding salt and pepper to taste and serve, add a dollop of Greek yoghurt to each bowl, the amount used is completely to preference
